My Interests

These are some of the research areas I'm interested in.

Computer & Network Security

With the increasing level of Cloud Computing and growth in network infrastructures, security has been my growing concern both as an individual and as a CSE student.I am always worried about my privacy and security. I would like to make it more secure and safe for others. I have always wanted to be a white-hat hacker! Cloud Security, Computer & Network Security, Biometrics, and P2P Networks are appealing areas of specializations to me.

Human Computer Interaction

Computers are part of our daily life. In as much as this is true though, we still have a long way to go to make it accessible for everyone and ensure that user satisfaction is guaranteed and the user feels safe and comfortable. UI Studies, Social Computing and Computational Cognitive Sciences are what I would be interested in pursuing. In addition to that, I would also want to contribute to non-disruptive and acceptable advertising in Social Networks.

Recommender Systems and Web Security

I love designing & developing website, hence a minor in Graphic Design. However, I would like them to be safe and secure, preserving privacy of individuals. I also want to study the current practices in designing the user interface to come up with newer solutions. Recommender Systems fascninate me and I would love to learn more about how they work and the concerns surrounding them. It is really interesting to look into the beautiful interwining of security, privacy and users.

About Me

The incredible journey summarized.

  • 2011 - 2014

    Humble Beginnings

    When I was ready for high school, I could not afford to attend a good school. I won a scholarship after scoring 97% in the admission exam. The scholarship covered full tuition for grades 10 through 12.

  • August 2014

    A significant step forward.

    After sitting for SAT and TOEFL, I applied to 4 schools in North America. I was awarded the MasterCard Foundation Scholarship for academic excellence and service leadership in March 2014 to attend my undergraduate studies in Michigan State University. I started studying Computer Science and wrote my first line of code ever.

  • Spring 2015

    Admission to the MSU Honors College

    I don't believe that success is a destination, rather I think it is a life-long journey. I kept on working hard and I managed to be top in the 10% of the College of Engineering students to get admission offer to the MSU Honors College. This challenged me to do a little more than required in my classes.

  • Spring 2016

    Admission to College of Engineering.

    I successfully completed the requirements for admission and got admitted to the CSE department in College of Engineering. I took my first programming class in Python and wrote my first line of code ever. Haven't stopped ever since.
